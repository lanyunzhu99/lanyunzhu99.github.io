About Me

I'm a PhD student in Singapore University of Technology and Design (SUTD), advised by Prof. Jun Liu. I'm also a technical consultant for the startup Motion Tech. Before that, I got the bachelor’s degree in June, 2020 from Beihang University. I also spent some wonderful times in Megvii, Sensetime and Johns Hopkins University. Currently, I am honourably sponsored by AISG PhD Fellowship Programme (one of the top Singapore scholarships).

My research direction is computer vision. At present, most of my works are focused on image segmentation. I’m also committed to the industrial applications of computer vision technology.
