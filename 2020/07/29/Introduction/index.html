Introduction

Posted on 2020-07-29

I’m a PhD student in Singapore University of Technology and Design (SUTD), advised by Prof. Jun Liu. I’m also a technical consultant for the startup Moxin Tech. Before that, I got the bachelor’s degree in June, 2020 from Beihang University and quit from the master project from the same school in 2021. I also spent some wonderful times in Megvii, Sensetime and Johns Hopkins University.

My research direction is computer vision. At present, most of my works are focused on image segmentation. I’m also committed to the industrial applications of computer vision technology.
